Meliha GÖZEK

Process Engineer


I am a process engineer and have a Master's degree in process engineering from Otto von Guericke University in Magdeburg. In my current job, I am working as a project engineer on energy conversion and hydrogen projects. I have also gained knowledge about the hydrogen approval process, laws, standards and regulations for hydrogen technology, emissions and fuel supply. My passion is to expand my career in the field of energy transition by developing effective solutions through data analysis and creating network infrastructures.


Besides my career, I am fond of physical pursuits, especially hiking, trampoline and archery. I also enjoy baking cakes as a hobby for myself, my family and my friends. I love traveling the world and enriching my life with new places, cultural interactions and food.


The works on this page are individual projects that I have created with my own endeavors and interest. The accuracy of the information contained herein has not been checked and validated by individuals or organizations. It is therefore only intended for personal use.
